FAQs

1. What is the importance of filing a design application?

Filing a design application is crucial as it legally secures your design, preventing others from copying or using it without your permission. It grants you exclusive rights to your design, protecting your brand's uniqueness.

2. How long does the design application process take?

The design application process typically takes 6-12 months, depending on the complexity of the design and the jurisdiction. Our experts at TrademarkIndia work diligently to expedite the process wherever possible.

3. What should I do if someone infringes on my design?

If you suspect design infringement, it’s essential to act quickly. Our Design Litigation services provide immediate legal support, helping you enforce your rights and seek appropriate remedies.

4. Can I file a design application for multiple designs simultaneously?

Yes, you can file for multiple designs, but each must be distinct and meet the legal requirements. Our team can assist you in managing multiple filings effectively.

5. What are the costs involved in design filing and litigation?

The costs vary depending on the complexity of the design and the nature of the litigation. We offer competitive pricing and transparent billing, ensuring you receive the best value for your investment.
